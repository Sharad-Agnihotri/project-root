1.Problem Statement:

Design an AI-driven solution that helps create, manage, personalize, or distribute digital content more effectively.


2. Objectives

Support ideation and brainstorming

Accelerate creative workflows

Improve quality and brand consistency

Reduce repetitive creative tasks

Preserve creative flow


3. Scope
In Scope

AI-powered creative assistance

Ideation, drafting, and refinement

Tone, style, and brand voice control

Feedback and improvement suggestions

Out of Scope

Full design tool replacement

Campaign execution

Manual reviews


4. Users

Writers → drafting & editing support

Designers → creative prompts & copy support

Marketers → messaging, CTAs & optimization


5. Functional Requirements (What the system does)
Creative Assistance

Generate creative ideas and variations

Assist with writing and rewriting

Improve clarity, tone, and readability

Summarize or expand content

Design & Marketing Support

Suggest copy, CTAs, and creative prompts

Generate design briefs

Adapt content for different audiences

Personalization

Support custom brand voice

Maintain consistency across outputs

Feedback & Workflow

Provide feedback and improvement suggestions

Save drafts and reuse outputs

Support user authentication


6. Non-Functional Requirements (How well it works)

Fast AI responses (3–5 seconds)

Simple, distraction-free UI

Secure data handling

High availability and reliability


7. AI-Specific Requirements

Use AI for creative generation and feedback

Allow prompt refinement

Learn from user feedback

Prevent harmful or biased outputs


8. Assumptions & Constraints

Users provide basic creative input

AI usage limits and costs apply


9. Future Enhancements

Visual and multimedia support

Team collaboration

Creative performance insights


10. Acceptance Criteria

Creative speed and quality are improved

Outputs match user-defined style and intent

System fits naturally into creative workflows
